ECE 495: AI Hardware Applications 1.5 (1) This course will require students to engage with Artificial Intelligence algorithms and frameworks to train and deploy machine learning models. Students will explore which types of problems are appropriate for AI to solve, data pipelining, hardware accelerated training, and inference on hardware and power constrained devices. Assignments may include system design, programming, and book reviews. Classes are organized as lecture and laboratories devoted to applying models. Prereq: CompSci 110. Sem hrs: 1.5
