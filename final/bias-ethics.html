34.4. Explore Bias With What-If Tool#

Compare two binary classification models that predict whether a person earns more than $50k a year, based on their census information. Examine how different features affect each models‚Äô prediction, in relation to each other. ~ UCI Census Income Dataset

  1. Go to What-If Tool, Getting Started

  2. Under Web dmoes select Compare income classification on UCI census data

  3. In the dashboard click Performance & Fairness

  4. Select Over-50K as the Ground Truth Feature

  5. Experiment with slices and optimization strategies.

  6. In particular, look at false positives and negatives

  • How is the Threshold adjusted?
